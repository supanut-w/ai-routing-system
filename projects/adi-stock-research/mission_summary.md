# MISSION SUMMARY: ADI Stock Research

## High-Level Takeaway
Analog Devices (ADI) has successfully transitioned into a system-level leader for the "Intelligent Edge" following the $21B Maxim Integrated acquisition. The company dominates high-margin Industrial and Automotive markets (76% combined exposure), providing a resilient moat against competitors like TI and NXP.

## Strategic Outlook
- **Financials**: Cyclical recovery expected in 2025 with record free cash flow.
- **Competitiveness**: Leadership in EV Battery Management Systems (BMS) and Industrial signal chains.
- **Risks**: Geopolitical friction with China (anti-dumping probes) and the execution of a 95% "Outside China/Taiwan" manufacturing strategy by 2026.
